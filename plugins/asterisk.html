Asterisk Plugin - asteriskstats

The multigraph plugin implements the following graphs:

  • asterisk_channels
  • asterisk_peers_sip
  • asterisk_peers_iax2
  • asterisk_voip_codecs
  • asterisk_conferences
  • asterisk_voicemail
  • asterisk_trunks
